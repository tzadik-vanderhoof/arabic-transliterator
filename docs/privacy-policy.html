Arabic Transliterator — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No information about you, your browsing history, or the pages you visit is sent anywhere.

How It Works

When you click the extension button, the text on the current page is read and transformed locally in your browser. The pronunciation dictionary used for transliteration is bundled inside the extension itself — no network requests are made.

Permissions

The extension requests access to page content solely to perform the transliteration. This data is processed locally and immediately discarded.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/tzadik-vanderhoof/arabic-transliterator.
